Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 24, 2026

Textis ("we", "our", "the app") is a word game available on iOS and Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

DataPurposeStorage
Device identifier (random ID) Identify your player profile without requiring an account Device (MMKV) & server (Convex)
Pseudo (nickname) Display your name to opponents in multiplayer Device & server
Push notification token Send game invitations and turn notifications (opt-in) Server
Game statistics & history Show your scores, win/loss record, and past games Device & server
Game preferences Remember your settings (sound, haptics, etc.) Device & server
Support requests Respond to your messages Server

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information. We do not require you to create an account.

2. Advertising

Textis displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information to serve ads. The app does not track users across other companies' apps or websites.

Where required by law (e.g. in the EU), a consent form (UMP/GDPR) is presented so you can choose whether to allow personalized advertising. If you do not consent, only non-personalized ads are shown.

For more details on how Google handles data, see the Google Privacy Policy.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — advertising (see section 2)
  • Convex — cloud database for multiplayer game data (Convex Privacy Policy)

We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or any other third-party tracking services.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties, except as described above (AdMob for advertising, Convex for game data storage).

5. Data Retention

Your game data is retained on our servers as long as your player profile exists. Since profiles are identified by a device ID with no account, you can effectively reset your data by reinstalling the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Textis is a general-audience app. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • Withdraw ad consent via the in-app consent form or your device settings
  • Request deletion of your server-side data by contacting us
  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the app

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data deletion requests:
